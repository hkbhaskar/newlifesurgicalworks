Easy Universal Femoral Nail - Standard Distal Holes

Zoom
  • Easy Universal Femoral Nail is used to fix diaphyseal fractures of Femur.
  • Dynamic holes are designed in an oval shape so that a screw at one end of the oval allows compression of bone and still controls rotation.
  • All the proximal and distal holes support 4.9 mm locking bolt.
  • The same Nail can be used for Right & Left Leg.
  • Instrument Set - AS032
  • Stainless Steel
