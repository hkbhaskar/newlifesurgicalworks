Condylar Plate 95° (With Dynamic Compression Holes)

Zoom
  • Condylar Plate 95° (With Dynamic Compression Holes). These plates are principally used for fractures in distal and proximal areas of femur, intertrochanteric valgisation osteotomy. It is designed to perform reconstructive osteotomies.
  • These plates have an angle of 95° between blade and shaft.
  • FFixation with 4.5 mm cortical screws and proximal two holes next to the blade is suitable for 6.5 mm cancellous screws.
  • The Two Holes next to the Blade are round and accept 6.5mm Cancellous Bone Screws..
  • The DCP Holes accept 4.5mm Cortex Screws.

  • Stainless Steel
  • Titanium
