TENS Elastic Nail

Zoom
  • TENS Elastic Nail - Length 40cm are intended for Diaphyseal and certain Metaphyseal fractures of long bones, Subcapital Humerus, Metatarsal, and Metacarpal fractures, and complex Clavicular fractures in Children.
  • In adult patients, TENS Elastic Nail is used for the Diaphyseal fractures of long bones in the upper extremity and Clavicle Shaft fractures.
  • (To be used with Instrument Sets AS149 / Q.149)
  • Stainless Steel (Elastic Nail)
  • Titanium (Elastic Nail)
