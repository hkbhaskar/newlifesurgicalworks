Indications

NET MIDFACE CMF Plating System is intended for use in selective trauma of the midface and craniofacial skeleton; craniofacial surgery; reconstructive procedures; and selective orthognathic surgery of the maxilla and chin.

Contraindications

The use in areas with active or latent infection or insufficient quantity or quality of bone.
